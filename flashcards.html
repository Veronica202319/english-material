Interactive Flashcards for Learning English

Colors

Red

Definition: The color of apples and roses.

Sentence: The apple is red and shiny.

Blue

Definition: The color of the sky on a clear day.

Sentence: The sky is blue and beautiful.

Animals

Dog

Definition: A loyal pet that barks.

Sentence: My dog is very friendly.

Cat

Definition: A small pet that purrs.

Sentence: The cat sleeps on the couch.

Fruits

Apple

Definition: A sweet fruit that can be red, green, or yellow.

Sentence: I eat an apple every day.

Banana

Definition: A long, yellow fruit.

Sentence: Monkeys love eating bananas.

Vegetables

Carrot

Definition: An orange vegetable that is crunchy.

Sentence: Rabbits like eating carrots.

Broccoli

Definition: A green vegetable that looks like a tree.

Sentence: Broccoli is good for your health.

Adjectives

Happy

Definition: Feeling or showing pleasure.

Sentence: She felt happy after winning the game.

Fast

Definition: Moving quickly.

Sentence: The car is very fast.

Verbs

Run

Definition: To move quickly on foot.

Sentence: I run every morning in the park.

Jump

Definition: To push yourself off the ground and into the air.

Sentence: The kids jump on the trampoline.

City Places

Park

Definition: A green space for relaxation and play.

Sentence: We walked in the park this morning.

Library

Definition: A place where you can read or borrow books.

Sentence: I go to the library to study.
