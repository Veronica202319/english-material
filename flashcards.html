--- layout: post title: "Interactive Flashcards" date: 2024-12-01 categories: flashcards --- # Interactive Flashcards for Learning English ## Colors 1. **Red** - Definition: The color of apples and roses. - ![Red](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/e/e3/Red_Apple.jpg) - Sentence: The apple is red and shiny. 2. **Blue** - Definition: The color of the sky on a clear day. - ![Blue](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/8/8b/Sky_blue.png) - Sentence: The sky is blue and beautiful. 3. **Green** - Definition: The color of grass and leaves. - ![Green](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/5/5f/Green_icon.svg) - Sentence: The park is full of green grass. 4. **Yellow** - Definition: The color of the sun and bananas. - ![Yellow](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/8/8f/Yellow_icon.svg) - Sentence: The sunflowers are yellow and tall. 5. **Purple** - Definition: The color of grapes and lavender. - ![Purple](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/3/3c/Purple_icon.svg) - Sentence: I wore a purple shirt to the party. ## Animals 1. **Dog** - Definition: A loyal pet that barks. - ![Dog](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/4/4c/Dog_icon.svg) - Sentence: My dog is very friendly. 2. **Cat** - Definition: A small pet that purrs. - ![Cat](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/3/31/Cat_icon.svg) - Sentence: The cat sleeps on the couch. 3. **Elephant** - Definition: A large animal with a trunk. - ![Elephant](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/3/37/Elephant_icon.svg) - Sentence: The elephant is drinking water from the river. 4. **Bird** - Definition: A small animal with feathers that can fly. - ![Bird](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/8/89/Bird_icon.svg) - Sentence: The bird is singing in the tree. 5. **Fish** - Definition: An animal that lives in water and has gills. - ![Fish](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/d/da/Fish_icon.svg) - Sentence: The fish swims in the pond. ## Fruits 1. **Apple** - Definition: A round fruit with red, yellow, or green skin and a sweet taste. - ![Apple](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/15/Red_Apple.jpg) - Sentence: She ate an apple for lunch. 2. **Banana** - Definition: A long, yellow fruit with a soft texture inside. - ![Banana](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/8/8a/Banana_icon.svg) - Sentence: Bananas are my favorite snack. 3. **Orange** - Definition: A round, orange fruit with a tangy flavor. - ![Orange](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/f/fa/Orange_icon.svg) - Sentence: I drank a glass of fresh orange juice. 4. **Grapes** - Definition: Small round fruits that grow in bunches. - ![Grapes](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/7/74/Grapes_icon.svg) - Sentence: Grapes are sweet and juicy. 5. **Strawberry** - Definition: A small, red fruit with tiny seeds on its surface. - ![Strawberry](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/f/f3/Strawberry_icon.svg) - Sentence: We picked strawberries from the garden. ## Vegetables 1. **Carrot** - Definition: A long, orange root vegetable. - ![Carrot](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/a/a8/Carrot_icon.svg) - Sentence: Rabbits love eating carrots. 2. **Tomato** - Definition: A red, juicy vegetable often used in salads. - ![Tomato](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/4/4f/Tomato_icon.svg) - Sentence: I added tomatoes to the pasta sauce. 3. **Potato** - Definition: A starchy vegetable that grows underground. - ![Potato](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/7/7f/Potato_icon.svg) - Sentence: Potatoes can be boiled, fried, or baked. 4. **Broccoli** - Definition: A green vegetable with a tree-like shape. - ![Broccoli](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/e/e3/Broccoli_icon.svg) - Sentence: Broccoli is full of vitamins. 5. **Onion** - Definition: A vegetable with a strong flavor and smell. - ![Onion](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/7/78/Onion_icon.svg) - Sentence: I chopped onions for the curry. ## Adjectives 1. **Happy** - Feeling good or pleased. - Sentence: She was happy with her test results. 2. **Cold** - Having a low temperature. - Sentence: It was a cold winter morning. (Continue similarly for other requested categories like verbs, city places, etc., in the same format.)
