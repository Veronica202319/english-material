Singular and Plural Nouns

A noun refers to a person, place, thing, or idea. Singular nouns refer to one item, while plural nouns refer to more than one.

  • Singular: "dog," "book," "apple"
  • Plural: Add -s or -es to make plurals. Examples: "dogs," "books," "apples"
  • Some nouns have irregular plural forms. Examples: "child → children," "mouse → mice"

Examples:

  • I have one apple. → I have two apples.
  • The child is playing. → The children are playing.
