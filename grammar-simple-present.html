Simple Present - Affirmative Sentences

The simple present tense is used to talk about habits, facts, or things that are always true.

  • Structure: Subject + Base Verb (+ s/es for third person singular).
  • Examples:
  • I play football every day.
  • She works at a hospital.
  • They eat lunch at noon.
